About Us

Vision Infonet Inc.

Vision Infonet Inc.. is an Illinois-based provider of cutting-edge technologies and services for the healthcare industry. Our medical transcription, medical coding, medical billing, Electronic Medical Records (EMR), Paperless Office System implementation, and other back-office services assure maximum profit for practices as small as one doctor to as large as a hospital group with four hundred or more physicians.

Founded in 1999 by a medical doctor, Vision Infonet Inc.. has steadily grown over the past decade to serve over 2000 clients all over the United States. Our employee base spread out at our 4 locations, consists of 750+ specialized staff and managers dedicated in providing exceptional quality end-to-end back-office services to the healthcare industry.

With the ongoing trends in information technology where change is an obsession, time a limited commodity, and quality the hallmark of every business, Vision Infonet Inc.. has created its niche in the service of healthcare providers by proactively understanding the current needs of the healthcare industry and successfully fulfilling them by combining high-end technology with highly skilled professionals to make the documentation process simple.

Vision Infonet Inc.. functions as an intelligent electronic platform between Government on one side and healthcare providers on the other. We provide a complete technology backbone that delivers automated, compliant, transparent and secure processes to the medical facilities.

The Strength Behind Our Success: Our Expert Team

At the heart of our excellence lies a powerhouse team of skilled professionals, each bringing expertise in their respective fields to ensure unmatched accuracy, compliance, and efficiency in healthcare services.

  1. Medical Transcription Experts Our professionals, backed by years of experience in transcription and proof-reading, are led by medical and English specialists, guaranteeing precise documentation that meets the highest industry standards.
  2. Certified Medical Billing & Coding Specialists With deep expertise in claims processing and AR calling, our certified team—guided by dedicated account managers—maximizes reimbursement accuracy while ensuring compliance with state-specific regulations.
  3. Elite Coders Our coding team consists of CPC-certified professionals (Certified Professional Coders) accredited by the American Academy of Professional Coders (AAPC). They adhere strictly to NCCI (National Correct Coding Initiatives) and LCD (Local Coverage Determination) standards, ensuring compliant and efficient coding.
  4. Billing Professionals Certified by AAHAM (American Association of Healthcare and Administrative Management), our billing experts are highly skilled in accurate, HIPAA-compliant billing practices that enhance financial performance.
  5. Innovative EMR Software Developers Our technology experts, certified as MCP, MCTS, MCPD, and MCITP, develop and maintain our cutting-edge EMR software—a cost-effective, high-performance solution tailored to the needs of modern healthcare providers.

With a workforce comprising passionate, qualified professionals, guided by seasoned team leaders with 7–15 years of industry experience, we are committed to streamlining and simplifying healthcare operations.

We don’t just offer services—we deliver unmatched expertise, innovation, and reliability that empower healthcare providers to focus on patient care while we handle the rest.
