HOUSE MOVING TIPS

Best House Movers Near Me

PACK ONCE, PACK RIGHT

Packing is an extremely time-consuming task when moving. It needs to be done right to avoid any damage when moving and to know where everything goes while unpacking in your new house. All this can be avoided by hiring Toronto Express Movers to provide a premium packing service to you. This, in turn, will remove the hassle of packing and you can rest assured that all your belongings will get to the new premise safely. Speak to one of our friendly packing service team members about adding this service to your next move.

CHECK YOUR INSURANCE COVER

Accidents are something that no one wants to happen but oddly enough they still happen. Toronto Express Movers has fully comprehensive cover from one of Canada's leading insurance providers to give you peace of mind when moving with us. To ensure you stay covered, please purchase this cover from us at least 48 hours before the scheduled move date.

CLEARLY LABEL YOUR BOXES

Clearly labelling your boxes is essential. It can help our team put the boxes in the right rooms the first time around saving you time when it comes to unpacking.

THINK ABOUT ANY PETS & PLAN AHEAD

Pets are important to all of us. Moving can be an extremely stressful time for them. Please ensure to make arrangements to relocate your pet on or prior to move day as our team will not be able to move your pets in the back of our trucks. Before moving, please familiarise yourself with local and landlord restrictions on pets.

TAKE CARE IF LIFTING OR STACKING HEAVY OBJECTS

Moving is extremely strenuous as it involves a lot of heavy lifting. If done incorrectly, it can cause serious damage and injuries. Our teams are professionally trained to carry out all types of moves with the correct technique and procedures to reduce strain on their bodies. Do not take unnecessary risks - let our team help you!
