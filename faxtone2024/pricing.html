Plans & Price

We offer customized plans to suit the requirement of each practice and hospital.
Each fax line costs just $3.15/mo and $0.04/page of transmission.
Each fax line includes a free secure email account. Select a US local or toll-free.

Each Faxtone Account Includes:
  • US Local or Toll-free number..
  • Free MDofficeMail email account for secure sending/receiving of faxes through email.
  • Facility to send/receive secure faxes from Email.
  • Facility to access Faxtone Web-portal: You can send and receive faxes from here, customize your settings, add and edit users and much more.
  • Desktop Application: Send and receive faxes from your desktop.
  • Smart phone App: You can send and receive faxes from your iPhone/iPad, Android.
  • Business Associate Agreement We sign BAA with the users as required by HIPAA with regards to PHI that is exchanged using our service.
  • Telephone, Remote Desktop, Email, and Chat Support at no extra cost to assist clients for smooth takeoff with the account.
Each MDofficeMail email account includes:
  • MDVault: End-to-end encryption for security and compliance at recipient's end.
  • 5 GB of storage. Email filtering, Virus protection, and Spam protection.
  • Legal Archival upto 10 years. Msgs can not be edited or deleted. (Optional)
  • Intelligent Automation and all other features of MDofficeMail service (details..)
  • Secure access from web, POP, IMAP, SMTP, iPhone and other smart phones.
  • Customization with your own logo and banner. (for accounts with own domain)
  • Online Calender, File sharing, and many more features
  • Advanced features like "Safe recipient", "Forced expiring"
  • "Email Your Doc" - Anyone can initiate secure message to MDofficeMail user.
  • MS Outlook Plug-In - Reminds the user whether message is being sent plain/encrypted.
  • Personalized spam settings, personalized "Family & Friends" settings etc
