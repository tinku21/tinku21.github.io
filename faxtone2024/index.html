About Us

Welcome to Faxtone!

By simply accessing your secure email, you can view all your office faxes anytime, anywhere. In addition, you can send faxes from your email, web-portal. Faxtone secure fax service enables you to easily send faxes to multiple recipients, receive faxes from multiple fax numbers and customize all of your faxes. Best of all, Faxtone secure fax service saves you time and money while enhancing your faxing experience.
