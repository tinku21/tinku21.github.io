Vision Infonet offers high quality medical coding services to healthcare providers across US. With several years of expertise on both physician and reimbursement sides, our highly dedicated team of CPC credentialed coders from American Academy of Professional Coders (AAPC) ensure highly accurate as well as compliant coding services in accordance with NCCI (National Correct Coding Initiatives) and LCD (Local coverage decision and medical policies) as per set rules for different states across US.
We have certified coders with CPC (Certificate for Professional Coding), certified by the American Academy Of Professional Coders (AAPC) at our Indian office. These coders are with medical background i.e. medical graduates, physiotherapists, medical transcriptionists and dental science specialists.

The coders complete a comprehensive training program and are involved in continuing education programs, with quality and compliance being continually monitored via a Corporate Quality Assurance Program and a Corporate Compliance Program. Our Coders are extremely careful while coding for patient records. Utmost attention is given to quality to get reimbursement from the Insurance Company for the services rendered by the physician. Use the most up to date books and software like CPT, ICD-9, HCPCS, Medicare Edits to keep in touch with the constant changes in the coding world.

Our medical coding team uses latest version of decision support software like 'Encoder Pro and Coderite. Our team consists of experienced coders and senior physicians with several years of clinical experience in a vast array of medical specialties, and has their CPC credentials.
