Services

Art of LIVING

In pursuance of awakening humanism, the trust aims to conduct lecturers, Seminars, Training Classes, Group discussions to guide the people to make lives more meaningful, the feeling of oneness, loving other, serving fellow beings and sacrificing to the needy, resulting in a wealth of wisdom that will enrich the quality of their personal, professional and spiritual life.

Yoga

The trust takes initiative to arrange classes for yoga in different areas to make the individual to have muscles of iron, nerves of steel with sound mind in sound body.         

Meditation

The trust fervently believes in "all power within, with that power, one can do anything and everything". To achieve this object, the trust plans to motivate and conduct meditation classes to awaken the hidden potentialities to develop self discipline to have peace, happiness, serenity and tranquility. The meditation helps to make people very strong to face any situation/challenge.

Satsanghas

To make people's lives more peaceful purposeful, meaningful and still the trust will organize Satsangs in various locations for different age groups to instill and spread moral values among the people for the wellbeing of society.

Youth Programmes

The trust taken initiative to design youth programmes in such a way that to make them aware of their own capabilities and weaknesses and to channelize their inherent potentialities in the right direction for making themselves very strong both physically and mentally so that weaknesses such as fear-complex in thrown off eventually resulting in more self motivated, inspiring and energetic individuals

Protection and Preservation of Plant Community

To spread the importance and the need of plant protection and preservation and to increase plantations towards maintenance of eco- balance in the nature which is essential for the well being of the society.

Protection and spreading of Medicinal plants

To make aware of the importance and value of medicinal plants in the nature by conducting various related programmes for the better healthy society.

Conservation and preservation of Endangered species

The need of hour is to take all necessary care to protect Endangered species of plants and animals.

Care and protection of cow (Go Samrakshana)
"Gomatha" (Cow) is a highly sacred animal and now there is a large need of Go-Samrakshana (Cow care and Protection) by Establishment of cow sheds, specially for indigenous species of cows and to make the people know the importance and value of cow, cow milk, cow urine, cow dung and panchagavya for better health of Human being by conducting various awareness programmes

To Aid and Establish Orphanages, Disabled and Old age homes
Conducting Medical camps in Slum and Rural Areas
Aid and support to Ashrams and community centre's
