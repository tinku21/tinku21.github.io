SSSVSAM and NB Seva trust

The Cardinal Aim of the Trust is to awaken true humanism in the present day society by spreading the true sense of love which only can generate the feeling of service and sacrifice among the people to make them more civilized with clarity of mind, charity of heart and a spirit of co operation to bring in the real peace and bliss in the their life.

The trust also aims to take various measures to protect the nature for the well-being of the generations to come with more thrust upon protection of cows and plants.
