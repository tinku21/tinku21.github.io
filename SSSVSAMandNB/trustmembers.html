TRUST MEMBERS

Sri Manam Kodanda Ramaiah
Managing Trustee

He obtained M.Sc Zoology (Post Graduate degree) from Acharya Nagarjuna University. He began his career with teaching profession and retired as Prinicipal in the year 2015. Throughout hiscarrer he endeared by himself to all the students through exemplified, effective and demonstrative teaching with Principles and values. He discharged his duties to the utmost satisfaction of his higher authorities or management but also to his colleagues and students. He is highly committed duty conscious, dedicated and devoted to his profession and always feels that “work is worship” and service to man is service to god.

He inspired thousands of students of different categories by which they got motivated by themselves and made their path of education highly successful, purposeful and meaningful. Their success has made them to assume high positions in different organizations not only in India but also different countries of the world. He always says that his wealth is his students and his students serving the society with principles and values.

As a human being he feels that he has to take up some responsibility socially. His involvement in the social responsibility is mentioned below:

  1. He extended his helping hand to poor students by paying their educational fees and saw that their education aspirations are fulfilled.
  2. He extended financial assistance to “anadha saranalayas” for the purpose of feeding orphan and destitute children.
  3. He also extended his might to inspire poor students to make their path of life by motivating themselves to reach their goals and to lead their lives successfully, meaningfully and peacefully.
  4. He gave high importance to clean and green in the society by participating himself and encouraging his colleagues and students to involve in clean and green programmes, being conducted throughout his career.
  5. He is one of the trustees in M/s SWEEYA SEVA TRUST.  As a trustee he himself involved in advising, guiding and planning to arrange various programs such as
    • Conducting medical camps in slum areas
    • Distribution of medals, certificates to the students who stood in first three ranks in 10th Class in their schools (Govt School at Quthbullapur)
    • Financial assistance to AnadhaSaranalays for the purpose of feeding the children, paying the fees to their education and to their uniforms.
    • Organising Chalivendra’s during Summer season at various locations.
  6. Plantations and Plant protection programs were conducted to protect environment
  7. He also extended financial aid to maintain cows in Goshala’s.
