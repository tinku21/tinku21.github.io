Welcome to Our Restaurant

Indian speciality restaurant opens its doors for diners all days of the week

Indulge in evenings of culture, cuisine and conversations with curated dishes and live instrumental music which transports you to the splendor of Riyasati Rasoi’s of Hyderabad, United Punjab, Telangana in an authentic fashion - inspired by Persian and Afghani cooking. Bidri retains the class and brilliance of the Nizami kitchens with secret recipes inherited from one generation to another, taking the unforgettable dining experience a notch higher. Chef Kamran Khan takes charge and the journey continues as Bidri adds a few more jewels to its crown and brings you a line-up of splendid dishes all through the week Monday to Sunday.
